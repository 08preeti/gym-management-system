How to track your workouts

Last updated: Oct 24, 2023 • 4 min read

Introduction

Tracking your workouts is essential for staying consistent and reaching your fitness goals. Our app makes it easy to log every set, rep, and weight so you can see your progress over time.

Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to start tracking your first session:

  • 1 Open the Workouts tab from the bottom navigation bar.
  • 2 Select a pre-built routine or tap "Start Empty Workout" to build your own.
  • 3 Add exercises by searching our library of over 500 movements.
  • 4 Enter your weight and reps for each set. The app will automatically calculate your 1RM (One Rep Max).
lightbulb

Pro Tip

Use the "Rest Timer" feature between sets to ensure optimal recovery. You can customize the timer length for each individual exercise.

Ending Your Session

When you're finished, tap the "Finish" button in the top right corner. You'll see a summary of your performance, including personal records and total volume lifted. Your data will be synced across all your devices automatically.
